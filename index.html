Inside the plant

A continuous line, from raw pipe to finished fitting.

Our Sfax facility runs a fully integrated process: hot induction bending, cold pressing, calibration, normalization, shot-blasting, marking and packaging — all under a single quality plan, with traceability at every stage.

Cold forming

Seamless & welded pipe shaped under hydraulic press.

Normalization

Heat treatment to relieve stress & restore microstructure.

Inspection

Dimensional, hardness, NDT & hydrostatic on request.

Marking & pack

Micro-percussion stamping, palletizing, MTC 3.1.

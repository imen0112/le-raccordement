Pipe Reducers.

Concentric reducers (Form 1 & Form 2) and eccentric reducers connecting two pipe diameters. Cold-formed from seamless or welded pipe, normalized and supplied with EN 10204 3.1 certificate.

ASME B16.9EN 10253-1 / -2DIN 2616 DN20×DN15 – DN200×DN100
Concentric vs eccentric

Same job, different geometry.

A concentric reducer keeps both pipes on the same centerline — used in vertical risers and most general pipework. An eccentric reducer offsets the centerlines so one outer surface stays flush — critical on horizontal pump suction lines, where air pockets must be eliminated.

Concentric

Coaxial bores. Use on vertical lines and balanced flow paths.

Eccentric

Offset bores. Horizontal pump suction — no air-pocket risk.

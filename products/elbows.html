Pipe Elbows.

Long-radius 45°, 90° and 180° elbows in 1.5D and 3D bend radius. Cold-formed from seamless or welded pipe in carbon and stainless grades — every fitting normalized, shot-blasted, marked and shipped with EN 10204 3.1 Mill Test Certificate.

ASME B16.9EN 10253-1 / -2DIN 2605 DN15 – DN300½″ – 12″
Why long-radius

Engineered for flow.

A long-radius elbow keeps fluid moving — its 1.5D (radius = 1.5 × nominal diameter) or 3D bend reduces turbulence and pressure drop versus a tight short-radius bend. That matters in slurry lines, high-velocity gas transport, and any system where erosion or pumping cost compounds over kilometers of pipe.

1.5D radius

Compact bend for standard pipework — saves space, fits most ASME specs.

3D radius

Wider sweep, less pressure drop — ideal for transport & high-velocity lines.

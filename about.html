Our mission

"Make the best product possible."

That was the founding idea — and it still drives everything we manufacture. Le Raccordement was created to bring world-class pipe-fittings production to North Africa: same specifications as the best European mills, made closer to the projects that need them.

Today we supply industrial buyers in Tunisia and across Europe — from individual EPC contractors to large public-works projects, and specialist distributors in France and Italy. Every fitting we ship carries the same promise: traceable raw material, audited process, full certification.

How we work

From raw pipe to finished fitting.

A continuous, integrated process — no outsourcing, no compromise on traceability.

Raw material

Seamless or welded pipe sourced from qualified mills with full chemical & mechanical certs.

Cold forming

Hydraulic press shapes the pipe into the target fitting geometry without compromising grain structure.

Normalization

Full heat treatment to relieve residual stress and restore microstructure to spec.

Calibration

Each piece sized, faced and chamfered to ASME B16.9 / EN 10253 dimensional standards.

Inspection

Dimensional, hardness, NDT — and hydrostatic on request. Reports archived per heat number.

Finishing

Shot-blasting, painting or hot-dip galvanizing — to customer specification.

Marking

Micro-percussion stamping with size, schedule, grade, batch and heat for full traceability.

Packing & dispatch

Palletized or carton-packed for export. EN 10204 3.1 certificate enclosed with shipment.

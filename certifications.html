Quality & certifications

Audited quality.
End-to-end traceability.

Every fitting we ship is backed by an audited quality management system, dimensional & mechanical inspection, and a Mill Test Certificate matched to the heat number of the raw pipe.

Certification

ISO 9001 — Quality Management.

Our quality system is certified to ISO 9001 and independently audited by Bureau Veritas. The standard governs how we plan, control, inspect and continuously improve every step of production — from raw-pipe receipt to dispatch.

  • Audited annually by an accredited third party
  • Documented quality plans for each product family
  • Non-conformity tracking and corrective action records
  • Calibrated inspection equipment with traceable standards
Standards we manufacture to

Specifications, in one place.

Dimensional

ASME B16.9

Factory-made wrought butt-welding fittings. The North-American reference for pipe-fitting dimensions and tolerances.

Dimensional

EN 10253-1 & -2

European standard for butt-welding pipe fittings. Part 1 covers carbon steel without inspection, Part 2 with full inspection.

Dimensional

DIN 2605 / 2615 / 2616 / 2617

German dimensional standards — DIN 2605 (elbows), 2615 (tees), 2616 (reducers), 2617 (caps).

Material

ASTM A234 / A234M

Chemical & mechanical requirements for piping fittings in carbon and ferritic steels — covers our WPB grade.

Material

ASTM A403 / A403M

Austenitic stainless steel fittings — WP304L, WP316L grades.

Material

ASTM A106 · API 5L

Seamless steel hot-rolled pipe — our primary raw material specifications.

Wall

ASME B36.10M

Welded and seamless wrought-steel pipe dimensions — schedules STD, XS, 40, 80 and beyond.

Certificate

EN 10204 — 3.1 / 2.2

Mill Test Certificate (3.1) or Test Report (2.2). Issued with every shipment, matched to raw-material heat number.

Audit

Bureau Veritas

Independent third-party audit and product certification. Inspection on request for critical-service orders.

Inspection & testing

All the tests, in-house.

From dimensional checks to hydrostatic pressure testing — we run the full inspection suite on-site and archive the reports by heat number.

Dimensional inspection

Outside diameter, wall thickness, centre-to-end, angularity, ovality — to ASME B16.9 tolerances.

Hardness test

Brinell / Vickers hardness verification on every batch — matches certified raw-material values.

Impact test (Charpy)

Low-temperature impact toughness on request — typical for cold-service piping.

NDT — DP, MT, UT

Dye-penetrant, magnetic-particle and ultrasonic testing — full reports issued per heat.

Metallographic test

Microstructure verification after normalization — confirms heat-treatment effectiveness.

Hydrostatic test

Pressure-tested to 1.5× design pressure on request — full hydrostatic test report supplied.
